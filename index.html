How to Play

Build a colony by feeding workers and letting them excavate tunnels underground. A queen in the nest lays eggs that progress through larva and pupa into workers, soldiers, breeders, and males. Use surface foraging and underground expansion together.

Mouse

  • Left drag: paint selected tool
  • Right drag / Shift+drag: pan camera
  • Mouse wheel: zoom

Keybindings

  • Space: Pause/Resume
  • N: Step one tick
  • R: Reset from seed
  • 1..6: Select tool (food, wall, water, hazard, erase, nest)
  • F/G/D: Toggle to-food/to-home/danger overlays
  • O: Toggle food overlay
  • U: Toggle underground/surface view
  • H: Toggle this help panel
